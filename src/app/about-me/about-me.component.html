Want to hire me?

If you like what you've read about me and wish to get in contact about a job, here are some of my preferences and things to keep in mind. Just like if I was applying for a job I would need to reference aspects of the job posting, I ask that if you initiate contact, that you do so with references to as many of these points as possible.

  • Intermediate Level - I have a lot of recruiters contacting me about junior or senior positions and then act surprised when I tell them I'm over / under qualified. If you have a position like this, but the experience level can be flexible to include intermediate, then be sure to mention that.
  • Salary - Be up front with what I can expect given my current experience level. I will not disclose my current salary if asked.
  • Age of the product - I personally found that I really like the experience of working on a young product. I like being involved in decision making early and and helping plan things out. It also helps to not have to work on someone else's 15 year old code.
  • Who will be interviewing me - I have no interest in going through multiple interviews with HR reps before talking to someone technical. The sooner I can speak with whoever will be in charge of the advertised role, the better.
  • Location - I currently HEAVILY favour remote work but I am willing to work in person depending on other factors, such as salary and location. I am open to relocating, including to Australia, for the right role.
