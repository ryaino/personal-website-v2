Want to hire me?

If you like what you've read about me and wish to get in contact about a job, here are some of my preferences and what I would be looking for in my next role. I don't require any all of these but the more boxes that are ticked, the better. Just like if I was applying for a job I would need to reference aspects of the job posting, I ask that if you initiate contact, that you do so with references to as many of these points as possible.

  • Intermediate Level - I have a lot of recruiters contacting me about junior or senior positions and then act surprised when I tell them I'm over / under qualified. If you have a position like this, but the experience level can be flexible to include intermediate, then be sure to mention that.
  • Salary - Be up front with what I can expect given my current experience level. I will not disclose my current salary if asked.
  • Location - I currently HEAVILY favour remote work but I am willing to work in person depending on other factors, such as salary and location. I am open to relocating, including to Australia, for the right role.
  • Product Age - The younger the product the better. I love learning about new and modern technologies, as well as the whole process of planning a project as early as possible. Being able to get in at the ground floor and have some influence on decisions would be great.
  • Industry - Working in an industry that appeals to me and that I'm passionate about is really important to me. I know this is very broad and hard to quantify, but the best way to put it is that I have no interest in industries such as banking or accounting for example. Somewhere that can have a positive impact on society or related to a personal hobby would be ideal.
  • Co workers - Throughout my entire career I have always been the least experienced person at every job. I remember how much I struggled when I first started my career so I would love to be on a team where I can mentor or have the opportunity to help some more junior devs.
  • Career Progression - What are the promotional paths from the initial role? Where can I expect to be in 1, 2, 5 years? I have a natural desire to gravitate towards leadership positions and would jump at any opportunity where that would be a possibility.
  • AWS - Ideally I would like to avoid anywhere that uses AWS for anything. The way Amazon employees are treated is criminal and I strongly support using any alternatives to their products.
